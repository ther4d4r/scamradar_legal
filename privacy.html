Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 19, 2025

ScamRadar is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect and use your data.

1. What We Collect

  • Email address (for authentication via Supabase)
  • User-generated content (scam reports, descriptions, categories)
  • Approximate location (to show nearby scams and allow reporting)
  • App preferences stored locally on your device

We do not collect sensitive personal information unless explicitly provided by you.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To allow reporting and browsing of scam incidents
  • To display relevant nearby reports based on location
  • To improve the service and user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Supabase for authentication and data storage
  • Google Maps / OpenStreetMap for map tiles and geolocation services

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

Reports and related data are stored securely in our Supabase database. Preferences remain on your device.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated. Continued use of the app implies acceptance.
